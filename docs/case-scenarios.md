# Scenarios

Use the context signals to build transitions in your demo. These scenarios demonstrate how the interface adapts to different emotional states and market conditions. Users create their own accounts via the simulator.

## Crash Day
Markets open flat. Over the course of a few hours, bad news breaks, holdings drop, and the investor keeps coming back to check. Volatility rises, the portfolio falls, and the gap between sessions gets shorter. **How does the interface respond differently at each stage?**

## Returning After Absence
The investor hasn't opened the app in three months. Their portfolio grew, they received dividends, and some orders expired. They tap the app for the first time in 94 days. The next day they come back again, now caught up and looking for opportunities. **How does the experience change across those two visits?**

## Quiet Week
The investor opens the app Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday. Markets are flat. Nothing is happening. They keep checking anyway. **How does the interface handle four days of nothing?**
