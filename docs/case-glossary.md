# Quick Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Stock** | You own a piece of one company. Higher risk, higher reward. |
| **ETF** | A basket of hundreds of stocks bundled together. Lower risk because it's diversified. |
| **Volatility** | How much prices are swinging. High = scary. Low = calm. |
| **P/E ratio** | Is the stock expensive? High (>30) = pricey/hyped. Low (<15) = cheap/boring. |
| **Dividend** | Cash a company pays you for holding the stock. Like a small quarterly bonus. |
